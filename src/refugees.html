Ukraine Refugee Information

by AXTMoon

Information

Ever since Russia started its unprovoked invasion of Ukraine, There have been thousands of refugees have been fleeing the country on large trains or by foot.
But this hasn't changed much there have even been reports of Russians bombing train stations or setting up blockades more than 70 children have already been killed.
Some countries like the United Kingdom have said that they will be giving ruffly 300 British Pounds to anyone who will take Ukraine refugees into their homes.
As of March 13, 2022, more than 2.4 million Ukrainian refugees have been displaced.

Statistics

 The UN says that as of 9 March:
Poland has taken in 1,412,502 refugees
Hungary 214,160
Slovakia 165,199
Russia 97,098
Romania 84,671
Moldova 82,762
Belarus 765
More than 255,000 people have gone to other European countries, the UN says.
